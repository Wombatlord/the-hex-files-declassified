A glossary of terms relevant to the setting of **The HEX Files**.

---
## Aethria

The World.

---
## Age of Miracles

A mythological age in which the world is said to have been created and the various races created by Gods. Ancient even relative to earliest historical records. Access to truly powerful magic and cosmological harmony are said to have been lost with the end of this age.

---
## Age of Mourning

When record keeping started. A cataclysmic age following the supposed [[#Age of Miracles]]. The grand mythic sagas describing the [[#Age of Miracles]] are predominantly thought to have been written in this period.

---
## ARCANET

Arcane technology originally developed by governmental and military research interests. Comprises many interconnected networks of arcane systems used to facilitate the storage and exchange of information.

---
## Arcanotech

The name for technology partially powered or enabled by the arcane. Roughly equivalent to things available around the 90s / 2000s.

---
## Awakened

Modern individuals who have either begun to develop their magical capabilities, or become aware of the possibilities of magic, beyond what society accepts as possible.

---
## The Veil

A term amongst the [[#Awakened]] and the conspiracy minded for the alleged lies and manipulations deployed by governments and their agencies to supress deeper knowledge of the nature of reality.

---
## Unawakened

Average citizens that make up the majority of people in Aethria. Generally disbelieving of the capabilities of magic to do much beyond providing minor convenience or small simple effects. Usually consider any suggestions of other planes of existence to be nonsense and hoaxes.

---
## Unexplained Magical Phenomena (U.M.P)

The umbrella term for events that involve magic beyond the "mundane" or the existence of extraplanar entities and locations. Typically treated as hoaxes by the unawakened. 

---
## Unified Republic of Lumia

The main power on [[#Aethria]]. A 200 year old republic at the forefront of [[#arcanotech]] research and development. Conspiracies circulate among the [[#Awakened]] that the instruments of government are actually controlled by shadowy and powerful forces behind the scenes.
