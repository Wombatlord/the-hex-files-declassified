# Forgotten Past

**[[Glossary#Aethria|Aethria]]** has experienced innumerable turns since **[[Glossary#Age of Miracles|The Age of Miracles]]**, the mythic dawn of time and sentient life. In fact, the only memories of that golden age are recorded in epic poems, mythic songs, tragic plays, and fragmented histories from the age that followed it. 

**[[Glossary#Age of Mourning|The Age of Mourning]]**. 

When the Gods went quiet and walked away from the world, their inscrutable reasons lost to time. When the glorious magic and divine entities recorded in myth receded behind **[[Glossary#The Veil|The Veil]]**, leaving the world and its people to fall into discord and disarray. 

In the time since that tragic age, the peoples of the world have long overcome ancient struggles, and largely only historians and cultural academics of the modern republics concern themselves with the study of stories from ages long past.

# Protect The Present

For the typical citizen unmoved by history, the world might be said to be in a new **[[Glossary#Age of Miracles|Age of Miracles]]**, with great industrialised cities and incredible technology providing modern convenience, partially powered by the arcane energy infusing the world.

**[[Glossary#The Veil|The Veil]]** however, lies heavy over **THE TRUTH**.

As far as every day people perceive, only a low degree of magic persists from ancient ages, and people are all too happy to ignore the unexplainable and write off mysterious happenings as hoaxes. The stories of the **[[Glossary#Age of Miracles|Age of Miracles]]** seeming grandiose enlargements of their own mundane magic.

Only those **[[Glossary#Awakened|Awakened]]** to **THE TRUTH** of the thread of history are aware that there is much more power to be had, and much more beneath, behind, and beside reality to be explored. 

In fact, **THE TRUTH** is actively supressed. Covert organizations of Awakened, protectors of the status quo, know **THE TRUTH** is a powerful threat to stability and perhaps even reality. 

Answerable only to the highest levels of government, their very existence shielded by layers of bureaucracy, identities shrouded in anonymity, they work tirelessly to monitor and respond to any occurrence of **[[Glossary#Unexplained Magical Phenomena (U.M.P)|Unexplained Magical Phenomena (U.M.P)]]**. Field Agents operate in secret, using their own Awakened abilities to ensure they leave no trace of **[[Glossary#Unexplained Magical Phenomena (U.M.P)|U.M.P]]**, or indeed themselves behind.

# Fight the Future

Naturally, not all agree with keeping **THE TRUTH** behind **[[Glossary#The Veil|The Veil]]**. Conspiracies abound on hidden forums hosted on **[[Glossary#ARCANET|ARCANET]]**, speculating that rapid technological progress is the result of reverse engineered extra-planar technology, or that an assassinated President *knew too much*.

Awakened activists, astute archivists, and arcane anarchists all work to wake up the world to the reality and potential of Magic, and to the worlds beyond **[[Glossary#The Veil|The Veil]]**. 

Insisting that the energy which bleeds into the world through the thin edges of reality is only dangerous to the ignorant, that waking the world and empowering the people can only be protective, these groups form a secret counter culture trying to publicise **THE TRUTH**.

Ultimately all **[[Glossary#Awakened|Awakened]]** know one thing: **THE TRUTH Is Out There**.
